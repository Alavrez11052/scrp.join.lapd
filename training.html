Your path from recruit to solo patrol officer.

Training Pipeline

1. Academy Exam

Pass the academy examination.

2. Ride Along I

Demonstrate proper uniform standards, equipment readiness, and professionalism.

3. Ride Along II

Show confidence and readiness for increasingly complex situations.

4. Final Evaluation

Take command of calls and demonstrate mastery of department procedures.

5. Solo Patrol Authorization

Begin solo patrol operations following successful evaluation.

6. Patrol Officer II

Advance during the next available promotion cycle.
