LAPD Training Process

1. Academy Exam

Pass the academy examination.

2. Ride Along I

First supervised ride along. Correct uniform, equipment, and professionalism are expected.

3. Ride Along II

Demonstrate confidence, preparedness, and independence.

4. Final Evaluation

Lead calls and demonstrate mastery of department procedures.

5. Pass Evaluation

Become eligible for solo patrol operations.

6. Patrol Officer II

Await the next promotion cycle for advancement.
