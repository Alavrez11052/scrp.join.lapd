About the Department

The Los Angeles Police Department is one of the most recognized law enforcement agencies in the world. Within Southern California Roleplay, the LAPD upholds the same values of integrity, courage, and community service.

Whether you aspire to work patrol, join a specialized unit, or serve behind the scenes in our Communications Division — there's a place for you here.

  • Integrity in every action we take
  • Respect for every individual
  • Quality through continuous improvement
