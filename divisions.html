Specialized Divisions

Explore Career Opportunities Beyond Patrol

The Los Angeles Police Department offers diverse career paths in specialized divisions. As you progress through your patrol officer career, you'll have the opportunity to apply for positions in elite units that match your skills, interests, and expertise. Discover where your talents can make the greatest impact.

Our Specialized Divisions

🔍Detective Services Bureau

Investigate major crimes and complex cases. Work as a detective solving homicides, robberies, assaults, and other serious felonies. This division requires advanced investigation skills and a keen eye for detail.

✈️Air Support Division

Provide aerial reconnaissance and surveillance support to ground operations. Pilots and crew members conduct helicopter operations, aerial pursuits, and reconnaissance missions across Los Angeles.

📞Communications Division

Serve as the backbone of LAPD operations by managing emergency dispatch services. Coordinate responses, process emergency calls, and keep officers informed in the field.

🛡️Gang Enforcement Detail

Combat gang violence and suppress gang activity across Los Angeles. Work in specialized units focused on reducing gang-related crimes and maintaining community safety.

⚠️Gang & Narcotics Division

Investigate narcotics crimes and organized crime operations. Conduct undercover operations, drug interdiction, and investigations into criminal enterprises affecting Los Angeles.

⚡Metro Division

Provide specialized tactical support for high-risk operations. Metro units respond to armed robberies, hostage situations, warrant service, and other critical incidents requiring tactical expertise.

🚓Traffic Division

Enforce traffic laws and promote roadway safety throughout Los Angeles. Manage traffic collisions, DUI enforcement, and traffic safety initiatives that protect our communities.

🎯 Your Path to Specialization

Advancement into specialized divisions typically requires:

  • Patrol Experience: Most specialized units require officers to serve at least 1-3 years as a patrol officer first
  • Proven Performance: Demonstrate exceptional skills, reliability, and judgment in your current assignment
  • Application Process: Submit applications during open recruitment periods for your chosen division
  • Specialized Training: Complete required training and certification programs for your selected division
  • Continued Professional Development: Pursue additional certifications and training to advance within your division

The LAPD is committed to recognizing talent and providing opportunities for officers to develop expertise in areas matching their strengths and interests. Your dedication and performance as a patrol officer can open doors to some of the most rewarding specialized assignments in law enforcement.
