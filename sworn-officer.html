Are you ready to serve, protect, and make a difference in your community? The Los Angeles Police Department is looking for dedicated, motivated, and courageous individuals to join our ranks.

As an LAPD officer, you will uphold the law and ensure the safety of our city, gain access to specialized units, receive professional training, and be part of a respected team committed to integrity, service, and excellence.

As an LAPD Officer, You Will:

  • Uphold the law and ensure the safety of our city
  • Gain access to specialized units including Traffic, K9, SWAT, Detectives, and more
  • Receive professional training and opportunities for advancement
  • Be part of a respected team committed to integrity, service, and excellence
Requirements
🤝

Responsibility & Teamwork

You must demonstrate a strong sense of personal responsibility and the ability to work effectively as part of a team in dynamic, high-pressure situations.

⚖️

Professionalism

A commitment to fairness, professionalism, and community service is essential. Officers are held to the highest standards of conduct at all times.

🎓

Academy Training

All applicants must successfully complete the LAPD Academy training program before being cleared for field duty as a patrol officer.

Specialized Units Available

After completing training and gaining field experience, officers may apply for placement in one of our many specialized divisions.

Traffic Division K9 Unit SWAT / Metro Detective Services Gang Enforcement Air Support Gang & Narcotics
