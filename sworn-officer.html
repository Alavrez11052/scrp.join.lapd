POSITION OVERVIEW

Protect. Serve. Lead.

Sworn Officers form the backbone of the Los Angeles Police Department. Officers patrol communities, investigate crimes, conduct traffic enforcement, and provide critical emergency response services.


Upon graduation from the Police Academy, officers gain access to advanced training opportunities and specialized assignments throughout the department.
