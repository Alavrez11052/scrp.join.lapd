Communications Division

Not a Patrol Officer?

Police Officer not for you? Try our Communications Division! Do you have what it takes to be the lifeline between officers in the field and the community we serve?

The Los Angeles Police Department Communications Division is looking for dedicated individuals to step into one of the most important roles in public safety.

Every second counts — and you'll be the voice that makes the difference. The LAPD Communications Division is the backbone of our operations, ensuring officers and the public are connected when it matters most.

☎️ Join Communications

As a Dispatcher, You Will:

  • Serve as the first point of contact for emergencies
  • Coordinate police response to incidents across the city
  • Support field officers with critical information in real time
  • Remain calm under pressure while helping those in need

The Backbone of the LAPD

Without dispatchers, officers go blind. Your role directly saves lives and keeps every officer on the street safe.

What We're Looking For
🗣️

Communication Skills

Clear, professional, and precise communication is non-negotiable. You will relay critical information under stress where clarity can mean the difference between life and death.

⚡

Multitasking Ability

Handling multiple simultaneous calls, tracking officer positions, and coordinating responses — all at once. You must thrive in fast-paced, high-demand environments.

🧠

Problem Solving

Strong problem-solving and decision-making abilities are crucial. You'll assess situations rapidly and direct resources to where they're needed most.

🤝

Teamwork & Community

A deep commitment to teamwork and community safety. The communications center never sleeps — cohesion within the team is everything.

Why Communications?

Be the Voice Behind the Badge

Every call you take, every unit you dispatch, every life you help — it all starts with you. The Communications Division is where split-second decisions happen around the clock, and your skills are what make it possible.
