Communications Division

Police Officer not for you? Try our Communications Division!

Do you have what it takes to be the lifeline between officers in the field and the community we serve? The Los Angeles Police Department Communications Division is looking for dedicated individuals to step into one of the most important roles in public safety. Every second counts and you'll be the voice that makes the difference.

As a Communications Dispatcher, You Will

📞

Serve as the First Point of Contact

Answer emergency calls and be the critical link between citizens in crisis and the resources they need.

🚨

Coordinate Police Response

Manage dispatch operations for incidents across Los Angeles, ensuring rapid and efficient officer deployment.

📡

Support Field Officers

Provide critical information in real time, keeping officers informed and safe during operations.

💪

Remain Calm Under Pressure

Handle high-stress situations with professionalism, helping those in need while maintaining composure.

What We're Looking For

Clear and Professional Communication Skills - Ability to convey information clearly and concisely in critical situations.

Ability to Multitask in High-Pressure Situations - Manage multiple calls, radio communications, and computer systems simultaneously.

Strong Problem-Solving and Decision-Making Abilities - Make quick, accurate decisions that impact officer and public safety.

Commitment to Teamwork and Community Safety - Work collaboratively with officers and the community to serve Los Angeles.

☎️ Why Communications?

Every second counts and you'll be the voice that makes the difference. The LAPD Communications Division is the backbone of our operations, ensuring officers and the public are connected when it matters most. As a dispatcher, you'll play a vital role in saving lives and protecting our community. You won't be on the frontlines, but you'll be supporting those who are. Your decisions and actions directly impact the safety of Los Angeles. Join a team of dedicated professionals who understand the importance of communication, coordination, and care.
