Communications Division

Police Officer not for you? Try our Communications Division!

Do you have what it takes to be the lifeline between officers in the field and the community we serve?

As a Communications Dispatcher, you will:

  • Serve as the first point of contact for emergencies.
  • Coordinate police response to incidents across the city.
  • Support field officers with critical information in real time.
  • Remain calm under pressure while helping those in need.

What We're Looking For

  • Clear and professional communication skills.
  • Ability to multitask in high-pressure situations.
  • Strong problem-solving and decision-making abilities.
  • Commitment to teamwork and community safety.

Why Communications?

Every second counts — and you'll be the voice that makes the difference. The Communications Division is the backbone of our operations.
